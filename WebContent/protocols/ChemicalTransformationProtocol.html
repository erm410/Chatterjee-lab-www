Chatterjee Lab: Chemical Transformation Protocol 11/21/2013

Reagents
Chemically competent cells
Plasmid DNA
LB Media (1ml/transformation)

Protocol

  1. Add 1ul DNA plasmid to 50uL chemically competent cells on ice
    • Pipette up and down gently
  2. Incubate on ice for 30 minutes
  3. Heat shock for 2 min @ 37°C
    • One method: Use PCR machine with custom configuration
  4. Incubate on ice for 10 minutes
  5. Add 1ml LB
  6. Shake in 37°C incubator for 45 minutes
  7. Spin down tube and remove 900ul of supernatant
    • Resuspend in remaining 100ul
    • This step may result in overgrowth. Dilution might be necessary depending on transformation efficiency
  8. Plate on appropriate antibiotic
